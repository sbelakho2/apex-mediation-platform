# Glossary

## A

**Ad Unit**: A specific placement within an app where ads are shown (e.g., "Home Screen Banner"). Also known as Placement.

**Adapter**: A piece of code that connects the Apex SDK to a third-party ad network SDK.

## B

**BYO (Bring Your Own)**: A model where publishers maintain their own direct relationships and contracts with ad networks, rather than reselling inventory through a single mediator.

## E

**eCPM (Effective Cost Per Mille)**: The estimated revenue generated for every 1,000 impressions. Calculated as `(Revenue / Impressions) * 1000`.

## F

**Fill Rate**: The percentage of ad requests that are successfully filled with an ad. Calculated as `(Impressions / Requests) * 100`.

## H

**Hybrid Auction**: A mediation technique that combines real-time bidding (RTB) with traditional waterfall priorities to maximize yield.

## I

**Impression**: A metric counting each time an ad is displayed to a user.

## V

**VRA (Verifiable Revenue Attribution)**: A technology used by Apex to cryptographically verify that revenue data reported by networks matches the actual impressions delivered, ensuring transparency.

## W

**Waterfall**: A prioritized list of ad networks. The mediation platform requests ads from networks in the order defined in the waterfall until one fills the request.
